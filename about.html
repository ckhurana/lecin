Meet LECIN Family

We are a team of like-minded people with one goal: making a difference through holistic development.

Nupur Bhardwaj

She is the Founder, CEO and Managing Trustee of LECIN always helped others, even if it meant going out of her comfort zone. She is responsible for the capacity building of team members, fundraising & communication. She designs and facilitates sessions on language development. She is the go-to person for all problems and is also the face of LECIN. She's completed her B.Tech from Delhi University and post that has committed herself to service completely.

Ankush Panwar

Ankush Panwar serves as the trustee and COO of the organization. Having completed Economics Honours, he serves as one person who understands social beings. He is responsible for designing and facilitating sessions on literacy and library activities. He is the best friend to all the children!

Rohit Kumar

Rohit Kumar, the co-founder and trustee of LECIN is one who always keeps looking out for opportunities for growth of himself and the organization. He has experience in planning and conducting sessions with pre-primary children. He provides the creative head to the team.

Jyoti Devi

Jyoti Devi is our facilitator who assists the team in facilitating sessions at the learning center. She also takes care of community engagement programs. Having a great connect with the parents and the community members serves as a plus for her.

Kanchan Kumari

Kanchan Kumari is our second facilitator and she helps the team conduct learning sessions as well. She helps Jyoti Devi in conducting engagement programs for the community. She also converses with the parents regularly to keep them updated about the child's progress.
